INTRODUCE

A city where the past meets the present

Welcome to a capital city with over 3,000 years of history. With its rich cultural heritage, Beijing is a city that combines the classical with the modern. Beijing is rich in historical treasures, including both ancient architecture (Baohe Temple, Summer Palace, Forbidden City) and modern architecture (Mao Zedong Memorial Hall, Tiananmen Square). If you want to experience the market to the fullest, we recommend you to choose Panjiayuan Market instead of Xiushui Market, which is full of tourists. You are not a good man until you visit the Great Wall and remember to visit the longest man-made structure in the world. Tourism resources are abundant and the views are all magnificent. Beijing is easy to get around and shop, and there is plenty of good food.
